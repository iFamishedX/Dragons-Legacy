## Overview

Dragons-Legacy transforms the dragon egg into a central piece of gameplay by introducing an engaging capture-the-flag style minigame. Players compete to control the egg, earning points and rewards in the process.

## Features

- **Capture the Flag Mechanics**: Intuitive CTF-style gameplay
- **Dragon Egg Integration**: Makes use of Minecraft's dragon egg
- **Multiplayer Support**: Perfect for SMP (Survival Multiplayer) servers
- **Customizable**: Fully configurable for your server's needs
- **Rewards System**: Earn points and prizes for successful captures
